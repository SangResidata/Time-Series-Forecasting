# TIME SERIES ANALYSIS FAQ

## What is Time Series Analysis?  
Time series analysis is a statistical technique used to analyze data points recorded at regular time intervals to identify patterns, trends, and seasonal variations for forecasting results over time.

## What are the main components of Time Series Data?
Time series data consists of four fundamental components: trend (overall direction over time), seasonality (repeating patterns over set periods), cyclic variations (repeating but nonseasonal patterns), and irregular variations (unpredictable fluctuations).
